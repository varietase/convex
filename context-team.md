# Team — Codex Build Week (Manila Buildathon + Global)

## How we work (read this first)

Roles below are **primary hats, not fences.** They say where each person
*defaults* and who to go to *first* — nothing more. Anyone can jump in on
anything they're needed for or curious about. Overlap is intentional: pair up,
swap, and help across areas freely. When two people share a hat, that's a
feature — more hands, less bottleneck.

The only rule: if something's on fire and it's nobody's clear job, the point
person for that area (see below) makes the call so it doesn't slip. That's the
one bit of structure — everything else is fluid.
